Launched in 2006, mymobile was the first one-of-its kind large format specialist retail store that catered to all multi-brand smartphone gadgets in India. Over a decade since its inception, mymobile has almost become synonymous for all customer needs, with its tech-savvy staff, product range, Staged presence and the will to help customers.

Bringing alive the promise of a ‘Brighter Every Day’ for its customers, mymobile offers its customers a world-class ambience to shop both in-store and Staged at www.mymobile.com and also allows for a seamless ‘omni-channel’ shopping experience that lets a customer enjoy the best of both the Staged & the offline worlds. With 22,000+ products across 544 brands and 392 stores across 136 major cities of India, mymobile ensures that for each customer, a Brighter Tomorrow begins Today! From the newlywed couple setting up their house to the son reducing his mother’s burden with a smartphone; from the daughter gifting her parents a new SMARTPHONE to the newly promoted manager buying an smartphone to make the summer memorable by clicking pics, mymobile ensures that customers always get ‘More for their money’!

With this intent to delight customers all along, mymobile launched its own label products in 2008. Brand mymobile stands for – Life, More Beautiful; the promise is based not just on the beauty in the way they look but also the value they offer to a customer’s life. The range is a perfect fit, for those who desire a completely feature-packed and quality product.

About TATA Group


The Tata Group, a global enterprise, was founded in 1868 by Sir Jamsetji Tata. The group, with its headquarters in Mumbai, encompasses 30 primary business sectors across 10 verticals. With operations in over 100 countries across continents, Tata Group is determined to serve with the mission “'to improve the quality of life of the communities we serve globally, through long-term stakeholder value creation based on Leadership with Trust”. Standing strong to its mission, the Tata Group has not just established but also financed various institutions for researches, education & cultural enrichment.
Each company under the Brand Tata operates under the expertise of its own board of directors. With a collective employee strength of over 7,20,000, the group has around 28 publicly-listed companies. Some major companies under the group are Indian Hotels, Infinti Retail, Tata Advanced Systems, Tata Capital, Tata Chemicals, Tata Communications, Tata Consultancy Services, Tata Consumer Products, Tata Motors, Tata Power, Tata Steel, Titan, and Trent Hypermarket.
